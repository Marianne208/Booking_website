Recommendations - Things to do in Lyon

Basilica of Fourvière

Enjoy the panoramic view from this magnificent basilica perched on the heights of Lyon. A must-see for all visitors.

Parc de la Tête d'Or

Take a relaxing walk in this large urban park with its botanical gardens, lake, and green spaces.

Bouchons Lyonnais

Savor traditional Lyonnais cuisine in these small, typical restaurants known for their tasty and authentic dishes.
